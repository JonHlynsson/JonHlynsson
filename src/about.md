I am a licensed psychologist and PhD researcher at the Department of Psychology, Stockholm University, supervised by Professor Per Carlbring.

My research focuses on obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD), digital mental health interventions, and the role of artificial intelligence in psychological therapy. My doctoral work spans the classification of OCD, a multicenter randomized controlled trial of an augmented-reality-assisted exposure treatment for contamination OCD (ZeroOCD), and psychometric evaluation of brief OCD measures.

Outside the lab I am an endurance athlete — primarily a mountain ultrarunner — which shapes my interest in the psychology of performance, resilience, and recovery.
